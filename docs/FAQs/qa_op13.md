# Carrying On His Will [OP-13]


## OP13-001 — Monkey.D.Luffy

**Q:** My opponent attacked this Leader when I had 6 active DON!! cards; I used a 1-cost Event with [Counter], and the number of my active DON!! cards became 5. Can I then use this [On Your Opponent's Attack] effect to give my Leader +2000 power?

**A:** No, you cannot. The [On Your Opponent's Attack] effect would activate before you used [Counter].


## OP13-002 — Portgas.D.Ace

**Q:** If I activate a [Trigger] when taking damage, can I choose the order in which this Leader's effect and [Trigger] activates?

**A:** No, the [Trigger] effect will always be processed first. Upon taking damage, you'll first reveal your Life card and decide whether or not to activate the [Trigger]. This Leader's "when taking damage" effect activates after the [Trigger] effect is resolved.

**Q:** If I activate a [Trigger] when taking damage, can I choose the order in which this Leader's effect and that [Trigger] effect activate?

**A:** No, you cannot. In this case, the [Trigger] would activate first.


## OP13-003 — Gol.D.Roger

**Q:** If I have this card as my Leader, will 1 DON!! card placed during my first turn be given to my Leader?

**A:** No, it will not. If you have no DON!! cards on your field at the start of a turn, as would be the case in your first turn, the DON!! cards placed in the DON!! Phase are placed in your cost area as normal, and are not given to your Leader.


## OP13-008 — Emporio.Ivankov

## OP13-031 — Trafalgar Law

## OP13-032 — Nico Robin

**Q:** Do effects that read "cannot be rested" mean that the affected Characters cannot attack?

**A:** Yes, the affected Characters cannot attack. These effects make the affected Characters unable to perform any actions that require them to be rested, such as attacking or activating [Blocker].

**Q:** Do effects that read "cannot be rested" mean that the affected Characters cannot activate effects such as "[Activate: Main] You may rest this Character:" and other similar effects?

**A:** Yes, the affected Characters cannot activate those effects. These effects make the affected Characters unable to perform any actions that require them to be rested, such as attacking or activating [Blocker].

**Q:** Do effects that read "cannot be rested" mean that the affected Characters cannot activate [Blocker]?

**A:** Yes, the affected Characters cannot activate [Blocker]. These effects make the affected Characters unable to perform any actions that require them to be rested, such as attacking or activating [Blocker].

**Q:** Do effects that read "cannot be rested" mean that the affected Characters cannot become rested by the effects of your or your opponent's other cards?

**A:** Yes, the affected Characters cannot be rested by the effects of other cards. These effects make the affected Characters unable to perform any actions that require them to be rested, such as attacking or activating [Blocker].

## OP13-079 — Imu

**Q:** At what point at the start of the game do you resolve the effect "at the start of the game, play up to 1 {Mary Geoise} type Stage card from your deck"?

**A:** Effects reading "At the start of the game" are resolved after shuffling your deck and revealing your Leader card, after determining who goes first, but before drawing your opening hand. In this Leader's case, after deciding who goes first, the player using this Leader decides whether or not to search their deck and play up to 1 {Mary Geoise} type Stage card. If they do, they search their deck for the card, play it, and then shuffle their deck again.

**Q:** If both my opponent and I are using this Leader, which player resolves the effect, "at the start of the game, play up to 1 {Mary Geoise} type Stage card from your deck" first?

**A:** If both players need to resolve this "at the start of the game" effect, the player going first plays their Stage card first, followed by the player going second.

## OP13-119 — Portgas.D.Ace

**Q:** If I choose to return 1 of my opponent's Characters with a cost of 5 or less to their hand using this [On Play] effect, can my opponent then choose not to play a Character card with a cost of 4 or less from their hand?

**A:** Yes, they can.

**Q:** If I did not choose to return 1 of my opponent's Characters with a cost of 5 or less to their hand using this [On Play] effect, can my opponent still play a Character card with a cost of 4 or less from their hand?

**A:** No, they cannot. If you do not choose one of your opponent's Characters as the target of "up to 1 of your opponent's Characters with a cost of 5 or less" or if the chosen Character does not return to your opponent's hand due to another effect, your opponent cannot play a Character card with a cost of 4 or less from their hand.

**Q:** If I did not give 1 rested DON!! card to my Leader and returned 1 of my opponent's Characters with a cost of 5 or less to their hand using this [On Play] effect, can my opponent still play a Character card with a cost of 4 or less from their hand?

**A:** Yes, they can.

**Q:** I play this Character when my opponent's Leader is "OP12-081 Koala" then activate its [On Play] effect, leading my opponent to play a Character card with a cost of 4 or less from their hand. In this case, can my opponent activate the effect of their Leader "OP12-081 Koala" which says it can be activated "when your opponent plays a Character with a base cost of 8 or more, or when your opponent plays a Character using a Character's effect"?

**A:** No, they cannot. In this case, the player who played a Character using a Character's effect is your opponent whose Leader is "OP12-081 Koala".
