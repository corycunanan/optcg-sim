# Big Mom Pirates [ST-07]


## ST07-001 — Charlotte Linlin

**Q:** If I have 3 Life cards, can I use this [When Attacking] effect to add 1 card from the top or bottom of my Life cards to my hand, and add 1 card from my hand to the top of my Life cards?

**A:** Yes, you can.

**Q:** Can I activate this [When Attacking] effect if I have 4 or more Life cards?

**A:** Yes, you can. However, nothing will happen after you add 1 card from the top or bottom of your Life cards to your hand.

**Q:** Can I activate this [When Attacking] effect if I have 0 Life cards?

**A:** No, you cannot.


## ST07-010 — Charlotte Linlin

**Q:** If I activate this [On Play] effect and my opponent chooses "• Trash 1 card from the top of your opponent's Life cards.", which player's Life card is trashed?

**A:** The card at the top of the Life cards of the player who did not activate this [On Play] effect is trashed.

**Q:** If I activate this [On Play] effect and my opponent chooses "• Add 1 card from the top of your deck to the top of your Life cards.", which player adds a card to their Life cards?

**A:** The card at the top of the deck of the player who activated this [On Play] effect is added to the top of that player's Life cards.

**Q:** I activated this [On Play] effect when my opponent had 0 Life cards. Can my opponent choose "• Trash 1 card from the top of your opponent's Life cards."?

**A:** Yes, they can. In this case, nothing will happen because there is no Life card that can be trashed.

**Q:** I have 1 Life card and take 2 damage from my opponent's [Double Attack]. This is the Life card I check for the first damage and I activate its [Trigger]. In this case, does adding 1 card to my Life cards according to this [Trigger] occur before processing the second damage of the [Double Attack]?

**A:** Yes. You will activate the [Trigger] before the second damage, add a card to your Life cards, and then process the second damage.


## ST07-017 — Queen Mama Chanter

**Q:** I used this [Activate: Main] effect to rest this Stage and add 1 of my Life cards to my hand. At this time, can I choose not to add 1 of my Characters with a cost of 3 to my Life cards?

**A:** Yes, you can.

**Q:** Can I use this [Activate: Main] effect to add my Character with a cost of 2 or less to my Life cards?

**A:** No, you cannot. You can only add a Character with a cost of exactly 3 to your Life cards.

**Q:** Can I use this [Activate: Main] effect to add a Character card from my hand to my Life cards?

**A:** No, you cannot. When included in card text, "Character" refers only to Characters on the field.
