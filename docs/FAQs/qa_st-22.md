# Gear 5 [ST-22]


## ST22-002 — Izo

**Q:** If my opponent's Character attacks, and this Character is K.O.'d due to the attacking Character's [When Attacking] effect, can I still activate this Character's [On Your Opponent's Attack] effect?

**A:** No, you cannot.


## ST22-003 — Edward.Newgate

**Q:** If I use this [On Play] effect to reveal 1 card from the top of my deck and that card's type includes "Whitebeard Pirates", what do I do with that card?

**A:** In this case, you draw 2 cards from the top of your deck, including the revealed card.

**Q:** If I use this [On Play] effect to reveal 1 card from the top of my deck and that card's type does not include "Whitebeard Pirates", what do I do with that card?

**A:** In this case, you will return the revealed card to the top of your deck face-down.


## ST22-005 — Kouzuki Oden

**Q:** If this Character would be removed from the field by my opponent's effect while I only have 1 card in my hand, can I trash that card to prevent it from being removed from the field?

**A:** In this case, as you cannot trash 2 cards from your hand, you cannot use the effect, and this Character will be removed from the field.


## ST22-006 — Jozu

**Q:** If I use this [On Play] effect to reveal 1 card from the top of my deck and that card's type includes "Whitebeard Pirates", what do I do with that card?

**A:** In this case, you draw 2 cards from the top of your deck, including the revealed card. You will then trash 1 card from your hand.

**Q:** If I use this [On Play] effect to reveal 1 card from the top of my deck and that card's type does not include "Whitebeard Pirates", what do I do with that card?

**A:** In this case, you will return the revealed card to the top of your deck face-down.
