# 3D2Y [ST-14]


## ST14-001 — Monkey.D.Luffy

**Q:** Does this [DON!! x1] effect give +1 cost to Character cards in my hand or trash?

**A:** No, it does not. This effect only gives +1 cost to Characters on your field.

**Q:** My Leader is "ST14-001 Monkey.D.Luffy" and has a DON!! card given to it, so during my opponent's turn, because I have 1 Character with a cost of 8 on the field, my Leader has +6000 power according to this [DON!! x1] effect. During this same turn, if I no longer have a Character with a cost of 8 or more on the field, does my Leader's power become 5000?

**A:** Yes, it becomes 5000.


## ST14-002 — Usopp

**Q:** If this Character has a cost of 8 or more, can I use this Character's [When Attacking] effect to K.O. 1 of my opponent's Characters with a cost of 4 or less?

**A:** Yes, you can.
