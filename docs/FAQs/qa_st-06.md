# Absolute Justice [ST-06]


## ST06-002 — Koby

**Q:** Can I play this Character if I have no cards in my hand?

**A:** Yes, you can. However, you cannot activate its [On Play] effect.


## ST06-004 — Smoker

**Q:** This Character gained [Double Attack] according to its [DON!! x1] effect and is attacking. During this battle, the Character with a cost of 0 was removed from the field by an Event's [Counter] effect. How much damage will this Character deal?

**A:** If there is no Character with a cost of 0 on the field before the Damage Step, this Character loses [Double Attack] and deals 1 damage to the Leader.

**Q:** Will this Character be K.O.'d by the [On Play] effect of [OP01-094 Kaido]?

**A:** No, it will not.


## ST06-012 — Monkey.D.Garp

**Q:** Can I use this [Activate: Main] effect to rest this Character without trashing a card from my hand, or trash a card from my hand without resting this Character?

**A:** No, you cannot.
 All instructions specified before a colon are activation costs. Activation costs cannot be paid only in part.
If a part or all of the activation cost cannot be paid, then the effect cannot be activated.


## ST06-015 — Great Eruption

**Q:** Can I activate this [Trigger] effect if my opponent has no cards in their hand?

**A:** Yes, you can. However, nothing will happen according to the effect.

**Q:** Can I select the target to give −2 cost after drawing a card according to this [Main] effect?

**A:** Yes. Effects are processed in order, starting from the first effect written on the card.


## ST06-016 — White Out

**Q:** During the turn this [Trigger] effect is activated, does the effect preventing K.O. also apply to Characters I play afterwards?

**A:** No, it does not. The effect preventing K.O. only applies to Characters that have been played when this [Trigger] effect is activated.


## ST06-017 — Navy HQ

**Q:** Can I use these [On Play] and [Activate: Main] effects to reduce the costs of different Characters?

**A:** Yes, you can.
