# Egghead [ST-29]


## ST29-007 — Tony Tony.Chopper

**Q:** If this Character is KO'd when my Life is 0, can I use its [On K.O.] effect to add a card from my hand to the top of my Life, but forgo adding a card to my hand from my Life?

**A:** No, you cannot.


## ST29-008 — Nami

**Q:** If the top card of my Life cards is already face-up, can I still turn 1 card from the top of my Life cards face-up instead of my Character being K.O.d?

**A:** No, you cannot.


## ST29-013 — Rob Lucci

**Q:** Does the "total of your and your opponent's Life cards" mentioned in this [Trigger] also count the card that triggered the effect?

**A:** No, you exclude that card from the Life cards being counted.


## ST29-014 — Roronoa Zoro

**Q:** Can [Rush: Character] cards attack an active Character on the turn in which it is played?

**A:** No, they cannot. They can only attack rested Characters, as described under the regular attack rules.

**Q:** If a [Rush: Character] is played and attacks a character on that turn, can OP14-060 Donquixote Doflamingo's [On Your Opponent's Attack] effect be used to change the target of the attack to your Leader Donquixote Doflamingo?

**A:** Yes, it can.


## ST29-016 — Kizaru!! Compared to Two Years Ago We're a Hundred Times Stronger Now!!

**Q:** If a character with [Unblockable] attacks a character, can OP14-060 Donquixote Doflamingo's [On Your Opponent's Attack] effect be used to change the target of the attack to your Leader Donquixote Doflamingo?

**A:** Yes, it can. Changing the attack's target with an effect is different from using [Blocker] to block.

**Q:** If a character with [Unblockable] attacks a character, can OP14-060 Donquixote Doflamingo's [On Your Opponent's Attack] effect or EB01-038 Oh Come My Way's [Counter] effect be used to change the target of the attack to a character with [Blocker]?

**A:** Yes, it can. Changing the attack's target with an effect is different from using [Blocker] to block.
