# One Piece Heroines Edition [EB-03]


## EB03-003 — Uta

**Q:** If my Leader is not [Uta], can I still play up to 1 Character card with 6000 power or less and no base effect from my hand?

**A:** No, you cannot.


## EB03-004 — Carina

**Q:** If the [When Attacking]/[On Block] effect of my "OP06-009 Shuraiya" causes it to have 6000 base power or more, does this Character gain +4000 power with its [Opponent's Turn] effect?

**A:** No, it will not. In this case, because [Shuraiya] has 6000 base power or more, you will have a Character "with 6000 base power or more", and therefore [Carina] will not gain +4000 power.

## EB03-051 — Charlotte Smoothie

**Q:** I have a face-up Life card and my opponent has a Character with a cost of 2 or less. If I choose not to K.O. my opponent's Character with a cost of 2 or less with this [On Play] effect, do I still turn all of my Life cards face-down?

**A:** In this case, you would still turn all of your Life cards face-down.

**Q:** If I have a face-up Life card, and my opponent does not have a Character with a cost of 2 or less, do I still turn all of my Life cards face-down with this [On Play] effect?

**A:** In this case, you would still turn all of your Life cards face-down.


## EB03-055 — Nico Robin

**Q:** When my opponent uses this card's [On K.O.] effect, can I activate a [Blocker] or [Counter] from my hand to prevent the damage?

**A:** No, you cannot.

**Q:** Can I activate the [Trigger] effect of a card added to my hand from my Life cards when my opponent uses this card's [On K.O.] effect to deal damage to me?

**A:** Yes, you can.

**Q:** If this card has [Banish] when activating its [On K.O.] effect, are the cards added to the hand from the player's Life cards trashed instead?

**A:** No, they are not. [Banish] only causes cards added to the hand from the player's Life cards by damage dealt from an attack to be trashed instead.

**Q:** If this card has [Double Attack] when I activate its [On K.O.] effect, can I deal 2 damage?

**A:** No, you cannot. The effect of [Double Attack] only deals 2 damage when attacking.

**Q:** Can I win the game by dealing damage with this [On K.O.] effect if my opponent has 0 Life cards?

**A:** Yes, you can.
