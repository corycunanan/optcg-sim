# Emperors in the New World [OP-09]


## OP09-001 — Shanks

**Q:** During my opponent's turn, can I choose not to activate this [Once Per Turn] effect when my opponent first attacks, and instead activate it the next time they attack?

**A:** Yes, you can.

**Q:** Can I activate this "when your opponent attacks" effect regardless of whether my opponent is attacking with their Leader or attacking with a Character?

**A:** Yes, you can. Provided it's only once per turn, you can activate this effect regardless of whether your opponent is attacking with their Leader or attacking with a Character.


## OP09-009 — Benn.Beckman

**Q:** Can I activate the [On K.O.] effect of a Character that has been trashed as a result of this card's [On Play] effect?

**A:** No, you cannot.


## OP09-014 — Limejuice

**Q:** If my opponent's Character with 4000 power or less that has been selected as the target of this [On Play] effect comes to have 5000 or more power as the result of another effect during the same turn, can that Character's [Blocker] be activated?

**A:** No, it cannot.


## OP09-018 — Get Out of Here!

**Q:** Can I use this [Main] effect to K.O. 1 Character with −2000 power and 1 Character with 6000 power?

**A:** Yes, you can.


## OP09-022 — Lim

## OP09-030 — Trafalgar Law

**Q:** Can I use this [On Play] effect to return this Character itself to my hand?

**A:** Yes, you can. In that case, you can still play up to 1 {ODYSSEY} type Character card with a cost of 3 or less other than [Trafalgar Law] from your hand.

**A:** No, you can only set 1 of them as active.

## OP09-041 — Soul Franky Swing Arm Boxing Solid

**Q:** Can the [Blocker] effect of a Character that has been set as active using the [Counter] effect of this card be activated during the same battle?

**A:** No, the Block Step of the battle would have already ended, so you would not be able to activate a [Blocker] effect.


## OP09-052 — Marco

**Q:** When do I "trash 1 card from my hand" as part of this [Opponent's Turn] effect?

**A:** You trash 1 cards from your hand when this Character is K.O.'d by your opponent's effect during your opponent's turn.

**Q:** When this Character is K.O.'d by an effect during my opponent's turn, can I choose not to trash 1 card from my hand?

**A:** Yes, you can. In that case, you will be unable to use this Character's [Opponent's Turn] effect to play this Character.


## OP09-058 — Special Muggy Ball

**Q:** Which player chooses the Character to be returned to the owner's hand as part of this [Main] effect?

**A:** In this case, the opponent of the player who used this card will choose 1 Character with a cost of 6 or less from their field and return that Character to their hand.


## OP09-061 — Monkey.D.Luffy

**Q:** If I activate a [DON!! −1] effect twice during my turn, can I still activate this [Your Turn] effect?

**A:** No, you cannot. This effect only activates when 2 or more DON!! cards are returned to your DON!! deck at the same time.

**Q:** I activated the [Activate: Main] effect of "OP08-074 Black Maria" and returned 2 or more DON!! cards to my deck at the end of a turn in which I had not yet activated this [Your Turn] effect. Can I still activate this [Your Turn] effect at this time?

**A:** Yes, you can.


## OP09-081 — Marshall.D.Teach

**Q:** My opponent took damage during the same turn in which I used this [Activate: Main] effect, and their Life card was "OP08- 106 Nami". What happens to the "[Trigger] Activate this card's [On Play] effect." on "OP08-106 Nami" in this case?

**A:** In this case, your opponent can choose to activate the [Trigger] effect on "OP08-106 Nami", but because [On Play] effects are negated, nothing will happen and [OP08-106 Nami] will be trashed.

**Q:** Can I use cards with [On Play] effects in a deck with this Leader?

**A:** Yes, you can include cards with [On Play] effects in the same deck as this Leader. However, all [On Play] effects will be negated due to this Leader's effect.

**Q:** If my opponent plays a Character with an [On Play]/[When Attacking] effect on the turn after I used this [Activate: Main] effect, will my opponent be able to activate that Character's [On Play]/[When Attacking] effect?

**A:** No, they will not.

**Q:** If my opponent attacks with a Character with an [On Play]/[When Attacking] effect on the turn after I used this [Activate: Main] effect, will my opponent be able to activate that Character's [On Play]/[When Attacking] effect?

**A:** Yes, they will.

**Q:** I used this [Activate: Main] effect when my opponent's Leader was "PRB01-001 Sanji", and my opponent played a Character with an [On Play] effect the next turn. Can my opponent use the [Activate: Main] effect on "PRB01-001 Sanji" on that Character?

**A:** No, they cannot.

**Q:** While [On Play] effects are being negated by this card, are my Characters with only [On Play] effects treated as Characters with no base effects?

**A:** No, they are not.

## OP09-093 — Marshall.D.Teach

**Q:** Can I negate the effect "Under the rules of this game, you may have any number of this card in your deck." using this card's effect?

**A:** Yes, you can. However, even if your opponent has 5 or more cards in their deck that are the same as the card that had its effect negated, it will have no effect on the match.

**Q:** I used this card's effect to negate the effect of my opponent's Leader for one turn when my opponent's Leader was "OP03- 040 Nami". What happens if my opponent's deck is reduced to 0 during that turn?

**A:** In this case, your opponent will lose the game.

**Q:** If a Character that had its effect negated by this card's effect is K.O.'d, can that Character's [On K.O.] effect be activated?

**A:** No, it cannot.

**Q:** If the card I choose for this card's effect is also receiving effects from another card, will this card's effect negate those effects as well?

**A:** No, it will not.

**Q:** How long will the Character chosen as the target for this [Activate: Main] effect be unable to attack?

**A:** It will be unable to attack until the end of your opponent's next turn.
