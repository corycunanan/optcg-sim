# ONE PIECE FILM edition [ST-05]


## ST05-001 — Shanks

**Q:** Can this [Activate: Main] effect give this Leader +2000 power?

**A:** No, it cannot.


## ST05-005 — Carina

**Q:** If I have 1 card in my DON!! deck, can I add a DON!! card to the cost area with this [Activate: Main] effect?

**A:** Yes, you can.


## ST05-008 — Shiki

**Q:** Does "If you have 8 or more DON!! cards on your field" mean 8 DON!! cards including any given to my Leader or Characters?

**A:** Yes. All DON!! cards in your Leader area, Character area, Stage area, and cost area are counted.


## ST05-010 — Zephyr

**Q:** If this Character is attacked twice by <Strike> attribute Characters, will it gain a total of +6000 power during that turn?

**A:** Yes, it will gain +6000 power.


## ST05-011 — Douglas Bullet

**Q:** If my opponent has no Characters on their field, can this Character gain [Double Attack] according to this [Activate: Main] effect?

**A:** Yes, it can.


## ST05-017 — Union Armada

**Q:** Does this [Counter] effect mean that the Character selected will not be K.O.'d, whether by an effect or in battle?

**A:** Yes. The Character will not be K.O.'d by an effect or in battle.
