# Premium Booster — The Best [PRB-01]


## PRB01-001 — Sanji

**Q:** Can a Character with a cost of 8 or less with an [On Play]/[When Attacking] effect gain [Rush] using this [Activate: Main] effect?

**A:** No, it cannot.

**Q:** If I play a Character without an [On Play] effect and with a cost of 8 or less during my first turn and it gains [Rush] using this [Activate: Main] effect, can that Character attack?

**A:** No, it cannot.

**Q:** Can I use this [Activate: Main] effect to give [Rush] to "OP05- 005 Karasu", which has the "[On Play] If your Leader has the {Revolutionary Army} type, give up to 1 of your opponent's Leader or Character cards −1000 power during this turn" effect when using this Leader, which does not have the {Revolutionary Army} type?

**A:** No, you cannot.

**Q:** Can a Character with a cost of 8 or less and an effect such as "[On Play] You may trash 1 card from your hand:" gain [Rush] using this Leader's [Activate: Main] effect in the event that the activation cost was not paid and its [On Play] effect did not trigger?

**A:** No, it cannot.
