# Premium Booster 2 [PRB-02]


## PRB02-001 — Koby

**Q:** While I have 6 or less cards in my hand, if I do not K.O. my opponent's Character with 3000 base power or less with this [When Attacking] effect, can I still draw 1 card?

**A:** Yes, you can.


## PRB02-002 — Trafalgar Law

**Q:** If I haven't yet used this [Once Per Turn] effect, and this Character would be removed from the field by my opponent's effect while it has 0 power or less, can I still use this [Once Per Turn] effect to give this Character −2000 power instead?

**A:** Yes, you can.


## PRB02-004 — Jewelry Bonney

**Q:** I was attacked by my opponent's Character, and used this Character's [On Your Opponent's Attack] effect to set 1 of my DON!! cards to active. Can I then use the [On Your Opponent's Attack] effect of my Leader "OP07-019 Jewelry Bonney" to rest that DON!! card during the same attack?

**A:** Yes, you can.

**Q:** If I'm attacked by my opponent when I only have active DON!! cards in my cost area, can I choose not to use this [On Your Opponent's Attack] effect, use an Event or other card to rest 1 of my DON!! cards during that attack, and then use this effect the next time my opponent attacks?

**A:** No, you cannot. [On Your Opponent's Attack] effects will always activate when your opponent attacks, and even if you do not set 1 of your DON!! cards as active, you cannot activate it again for the rest of the turn.

**Q:** If my opponent's Character attacks and its [When Attacking] effect removes this Character from the field, can I still activate this Character's [On Your Opponent's Attack] effect?

**A:** No, you cannot.


## PRB02-005 — Monkey.D.Luffy

**Q:** I played this Character when my opponent had 7 or less DON!! cards and activated its [On Play] effect. Does my opponent still rest 1 of their active DON!! cards if they have 8 or more DON!! cards at the start of their next Main Phase?

**A:** Yes, they do.


## PRB02-006 — Roronoa Zoro

**Q:** When I use this [Opponent's Turn] effect, can I select a Character that cannot be rested due to an effect as the target of "you may rest 1 of your other Characters instead"?

**A:** No, you cannot. If you have no other Characters that can be rested, you will be unable to activate this [Opponent's Turn] effect.


## PRB02-010 — Charlotte Pudding

**Q:** If my opponent has 5 or less DON!! cards, can I still play up to 1 {Big Mom Pirates} type Character card with 6000 to 8000 power from my hand without drawing 2 cards?

**A:** No, you cannot.


## PRB02-014 — Sabo

**Q:** While I had 14 cards in my trash, I activated the [Activate: Main] effect of "EB01-042 Scarlet", which can be trashed to "play up to 1 {Dressrosa} type Character card with a cost of 3 or less other than [Scarlet] from your hand rested". Can I play this "PRB02-014 Sabo" from my hand using that effect?

**A:** Yes, you can.


## PRB02-017 — Boa Hancock

**Q:** Can I choose 1 of my opponent's active Characters other than [Monkey.D.Luffy] as the target of this [On Play] effect?

**A:** Yes, you can.
