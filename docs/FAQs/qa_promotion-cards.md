# Promotion Cards


## P-001 — Monkey.D.Luffy

**Q:** If this Character attacks on the turn it is played, does the attack become invalid if this Character no longer has DON!! cards given to it?

**A:** No. Battle processing proceeds as is.


## P-002 — I Smell Adventure Ahead!

**Q:** Can I draw a card if I have no cards in my hand other than this activated card?

**A:** No. As the number of cards you returned to the deck is 0, the number of cards drawn is also 0.


## P-004 — Crocodile

**Q:** After blocking, does the target of the attack revert back to the original target if this if this Character no longer has a DON!! card given to it?

**A:** No, it does not.


## P-005 — Kaido

**Q:** Can I activate this effect multiple times during the same turn?

**A:** Yes, you can.
In that case, this Character will gain multiple [Banish] effects, but nothing will happen.


## P-007 — Monkey.D.Luffy

**Q:** Can this Character be K.O.'d in battle by Characters without the <Strike> attribute?

**A:** Yes, it can. This effect only means that this Character cannot be K.O.'d in battle by Leaders with the <Strike> attribute or Characters with the <Strike> attribute.


## P-008 — Yamato

**Q:** Can I use this [Activate: Main] effect to rest this Character if my opponent has no Characters with a cost 2 or less?

**A:** Yes, you can.


## P-009 — Trafalgar Law

**Q:** Can I use this [On Play] effect to win the game if my opponent has no Life cards?

**A:** No, you cannot.

**Q:** If my opponent has 6 or more cards in their hand, can I choose not to activate this [On Play] effect?

**A:** No, you cannot.


## P-011 — Uta

**Q:** Does "Characters with no base effect" include Characters with only a [Trigger] effect and no card text?

**A:** No, it does not.

**Q:** Does "Characters with no base effect" include Characters with only a [Counter +1000] and no card text?

**A:** Yes, it does.

**Q:** I used a gain +1000 power effect of another card on a Character without card text. Is this Character included in "Characters with no base effect"?

**A:** Yes, it is.

**Q:** Is a Character to which a "Negate the effect...during this turn" effect is applied considered a Character "with no base effect"?

**A:** No, it is not.


## P-013 — Gordon

**Q:** Can I place this Character at the bottom of the owner's deck without giving 1 of my opponent's Characters −3000 power according to this [Activate: Main] effect?

**A:** Yes, you can.


## P-027 — General Franky

**Q:** What is "base power"?

**A:** The power value written on the card.

**Q:** If there are 2 of this card on my field, do my Characters with a base power of 3000 or less gain +2000 power according to this [Opponent's Turn] effect?

**A:** Yes, the Characters will gain +2000 power.


## P-030 — Jinbe

**Q:** Can I use this [On K.O.] effect to place my Character at the bottom of my deck?

**A:** Yes, you can.


## P-032 — Sengoku

**Q:** Does this [Your Turn] effect give −2 cost to Character cards in my opponent's trash or hand?

**A:** No, it does not. When "Character" is included in card text, it refers only to Characters on the field.

**Q:** My opponent's Character with a cost of 3 was reduced to a cost of 1 due to this [Your Turn] effect. Its cost was then further reduced to 0 according to the −2 cost effect of [OP02-106 Tsuru]. If this Character is then removed from the field or has all DON!! cards removed from it, what will the cost of my opponent's Character be?

**A:** In that case, the cost will become 1.


## P-033 — Monkey.D.Luffy

**Q:** I activated this [Activate: Main] effect when I had 1 card in my deck. In this case, will I lose the game because my deck now has 0 cards?

**A:** No, your deck will not become 0 and you will not lose the game.


## P-036 — Monkey.D.Luffy

**Q:** Can I use "This Character and up to 1 of your Leader gain +1000 power during this turn." to give both this Character +1000 power and my Leader +1000 power?

**A:** Yes, you can.

**Q:** Can I pay this [When Attacking] effect's activation cost ("You may add 1 card from the top or bottom of your Life cards to your hand:") and decide not to give this Character +1000 power?

**A:** No, you cannot.


## P-040 — Kaido

**Q:** What does "this Character cannot be K.O.'d" mean?

**A:** It means this Character cannot be K.O.'d by effects or in battle.

**Q:** My opponent has 10 DON!! cards on their field and their Leader has the {Animal Kingdom Pirates} type. My opponent activates the effect of "OP01-094 Kaido" ("[On Play] DON!! −6: If your Leader has the {Animal Kingdom Pirates} type, K.O. all Characters other than this Character.") and returns 6 DON!! cards to their DON!! deck. Can this [On Play] effect be used to K.O. this "P-040 Kaido"?

**A:** Yes, it can K.O. this card.


## P-048 — Arlong

**Q:** Does my opponent choose which card to trash according to this [On Play] effect?

**A:** Yes, your opponent, as the owner of the hand, chooses which card to trash.


## P-055 — Monkey.D.Luffy

**Q:** Does my opponent choose which Character to place at the bottom of their deck according to this [On Play] effect?

**A:** Yes. Your opponent chooses 1 Character on their field and places it at the bottom of their deck.

**Q:** Can I still use this [On Play] effect to trash 2 cards from my hand if my opponent has no Characters?

**A:** Yes, you can. In this case, nothing occurs for the "places 1 of their Characters at the bottom of the owner's deck" effect.


## P-057 — Fleeting Lullaby

**Q:** If I choose my opponent's rested Character according to this [Main] effect and that Character then becomes active during that turn, is that Character rested during my opponent's next Refresh Phrase?

**A:** No, it is not.


## P-058 — Where the Wind Blows

**Q:** After activating this [Main] effect, does my {FILM} type Character played during that turn and rested at the end of that turn become active?

**A:** Yes, it does.


## P-060 — Tot Musica

**Q:** Does "your [Uta] cards" refer to my [Uta] Leader or [Uta] Characters?

**A:** It refers to both your [Uta] Leader and Characters.


## P-104 — Shanks

**Q:** I have 10 DON!! cards on my field while my opponent has 9 DON!! cards on their field, and my opponent has this Character which costs 8 or less due to another effect. Can I activate "EB01-039 Conquerer of Three Worlds Ragnaraku" and use its DON!! −1 to change the number of DON!! cards on my field to 9, then K.O. this Character belonging to my opponent?

**A:** Yes, you can.


## P-106 — Monkey.D.Luffy

**Q:** Can I use this [End of Your Turn] effect when the top card of my Life cards is already face-up?

**A:** No, you cannot.
