# 500 Years in the Future [OP-07]


## OP07-001 — Monkey.D.Dragon

**Q:** Can I use this [Activate: Main] effect to take 2 DON!! cards currently given to my Character(s) and give 1 each to 2 of my other Characters?

**A:** No, you cannot.

**Q:** If 1 of my Characters already has 2 DON!! cards given to it, can I use this [Activate: Main] effect to give those 2 DON!! cards to that Character again?

**A:** No, you cannot.

**Q:** If 2 of my Characters have 1 DON!! card each given to them, can I use this [Activate: Main] effect to give these 2 DON!! cards to 1 of my other Characters?

**A:** Yes, you can.


## OP07-002 — Ain

**Q:** If my Leader attacks my opponent's Character that has a power of 0 due to this Character's [On Play] effect, and my opponent uses [Counter +1000] on that Character, does that Character's power continue to be 0?

**A:** No, the Character's power will be 1000 during the battle.

**Q:** During my turn, my opponent's "OP05-012 Hack" with a base power of 5000 is given +2000 power by the [Counter] effect of "OP07-018 KEEP OUT", making its power 7000 until the end of my opponent's next turn. If I use this [On Play] effect of my Character on that "Hack" card, will that "Hack" card's power be 0 during my current turn, and then 7000 during my opponent's turn?

**A:** Yes, in this case, it's power will become 7000.

## OP07-019 — Jewelry Bonney

**Q:** My opponent attacked with "OP01-060 Donquixote Doflamingo", then used its [When Attacking] effect to play "OP07-045 Jinbe". If my opponent then plays a Character using Jinbe's [On Play] effect, can I use this [On Your Opponent's Attack] effect to rest that Character?

**A:** No, you cannot. First, Jinbe is played with Donquixote Doflamingo's "When Attacking" effect, followed by the activation of this card's "On Your Opponent's Attack" effect. Then, Jinbe's "On Play" effect activates, allowing your opponent to play a Character from their hand.
