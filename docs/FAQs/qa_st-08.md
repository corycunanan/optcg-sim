# Side Monkey.D.Luffy [ST-08]


## ST08-001 — Monkey.D.Luffy

**Q:** When 2 Characters are K.O.'d at the same time during my turn, can I use this [Your Turn] effect to give 2 DON!! cards?

**A:** Yes, you can. You can give DON!! cards up to the number of Characters K.O.'d.


## ST08-005 — Shanks

**Q:** After I activate this [On Play] effect and trash 1 card from my hand, can I choose whether or not to K.O. my Characters with a cost of 1 or less?

**A:** No, you cannot. This effect will K.O. all of your and your opponent's Characters with a cost of 1 or less.


## ST08-013 — Mr.2.Bon.Kurei(Bentham)

**Q:** If this Character is attacked by my opponent's Character, loses the battle and is K.O.'d, this Character will be in my trash at the end of that battle. Can I still activate the [DON!! x1] effect?

**A:** No, you cannot.
