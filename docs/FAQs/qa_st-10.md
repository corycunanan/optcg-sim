# The Three Captains [ST-10]


## ST10-001 — Trafalgar Law

**Q:** Can I still use this [Activate: Main] effect to play up to 1 Character card with a cost of 4 or less from my hand if my opponent has no Characters with 3000 power or less?

**A:** Yes, you can.

**Q:** Can I use this [Activate: Main] effect to place up to 1 of my opponent's Characters with 3000 power or less at the bottom of the owner's deck without playing 1 Character card with a cost of 4 or less from my hand?

**A:** Yes, you can.


## ST10-002 — Monkey.D.Luffy

**Q:** If it is the start of the game and I have no DON!! cards on my field, can I use this [Activate: Main] effect to add up to 1 DON!! card from my DON!! deck and set it as active?

**A:** No, you cannot.

**Q:** What does "you have 0 DON!! cards on your field" mean?

**A:** It means you have no DON!! cards either in your cost area or on your field.


## ST10-004 — Sanji

**Q:** I played this Character and gained [Rush] from this [On Play] effect, and then the situation changed during that turn such that my opponent no longer has a Character with 5000 or more power on their field. Can this Character still attack during that turn?

**A:** Yes, it can.


## ST10-006 — Monkey.D.Luffy

**Q:** Can I use this [Once Per Turn] effect to K.O. a Character other than my opponent's Character that activated [Blocker]?

**A:** Yes, you can.

**Q:** I attacked with this Character and my opponent activated [Blocker]. If I use this Character's [Once Per Turn] effect to K.O. my opponent's Character that activated [Blocker], what happens to this Character's attack?

**A:** The battle will end without moving to the Counter Step.


## ST10-007 — Killer

**Q:** Does this [Your Turn] effect activate if a DON!! card on my field is returned to my DON!! deck by the effect of an opponent's card?

**A:** Yes, it does.


## ST10-010 — Trafalgar Law

**Q:** If my opponent has 7 or more cards in their hand, which player chooses the cards to be trashed according to this [On Play] effect?

**A:** The player who activated this [On Play] effect chooses 2 cards from their opponent's hand face-down.


## ST10-011 — Heat

**Q:** Does this [Your Turn] effect activate if a DON!! card on my field is returned to my DON!! deck by the effect of an opponent's card?

**A:** Yes, it does.


## ST10-014 — Wire

**Q:** Does this [Once Per Turn] effect activate if a DON!! card on my field is returned to my DON!! deck by the effect of an opponent's card?

**A:** Yes, it does.

**Q:** Can I choose not to draw a card according to this [Once Per Turn] effect when a DON!! card on my field is returned to my DON!! deck?

**A:** No, you cannot. You must draw 1 card and trash 1 card from your hand.


## ST10-016 — Gum-Gum Kong Gatling

**Q:** Until when is this [Trigger] ("until the end of your next turn") effective?

**A:** It is effective until it has become your turn again and that turn has concluded.


## ST10-001 — Trafalgar Law

**Q:** I have no "Monkey.D.Luffy" on my field when I use this Leader's [Activate: Main] effect to select my opponent's "OP05-100 Enel" with 3000 power or less, and play a "Monkey.D.Luffy" with a cost of 4 or less from my hand. Can my opponent use the [Once Per Turn] effect of "OP05-100 Enel" to trash 1 card from the top of their Life cards instead of placing this "Enel" at the bottom of their deck?

**A:** Yes, they can.
