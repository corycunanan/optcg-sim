## OP04-004 — Karoo

**Q:** What happens according to this [Activate: Main] effect if I don't have enough rested DON!! cards in my cost area to give 1 to each of my {Alabasta} type Characters?

**A:** You will select as many {Alabasta} type Characters as you have rested DON!! cards in your cost area and give each of them 1 rested DON!! card from your cost area.

**Q:** Can I use this [Activate: Main] effect to give a Character a DON!! card that has already been given to another Character?

**A:** No, you cannot. This [Activate: Main] effect directs you to give a rested DON!! card from your cost area.
