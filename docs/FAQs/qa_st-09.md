# Side Yamato [ST-09]


## ST09-001 — Yamato

**Q:** I have 3 Life cards when this Leader with a DON!! card given to it is attacked by a Character with 5000 power and [Double Attack]. At this time, if I have 2 Life cards after I take the first damage, what happens when I take the next damage?

**A:** After the first damage reduces your Life cards to 2, this Leader's power will become 6000 according to this [Opponent's Turn] effect. However, the second damage will still be processed.


## ST09-002 — Uzuki Tempura

**Q:** If I activate this card's Trigger, can I decide not to add this card to my hand?

**A:** No, you cannot.


## ST09-008 — Shimotsuki Ushimaru

**Q:** If the card added from my Life cards to my hand by this [When Attacking] effect is a "yellow {Land of Wano} type Character card with a cost of 4 or less", can I use this effect to play that card?

**A:** Yes, you can.


## ST09-009 — Fugetsu Omusubi

**Q:** If I activate this card's Trigger, can I decide not to add this card to my hand?

**A:** No, you cannot.


## ST09-010 — Portgas.D.Ace

**Q:** If I have no Life cards when this Character would be K.O.'d, can I still trash 1 card from the top or bottom of my Life cards instead?

**A:** No, you cannot.
