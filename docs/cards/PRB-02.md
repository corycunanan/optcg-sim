# PRB-02

## Koby
**PRB02-001** · Character · Red

[Opponent's Turn] If your Leader has the {Navy} type, this Character gains +1000 power.
[When Attacking] K.O. up to 1 of your opponent's Characters with 3000 base power or less. Then, if you have 6 or less cards in your hand, draw 1 card.

---

## Trafalgar Law
**PRB02-002** · Character · Red

[Once Per Turn] If this Character would be removed from the field by your opponent's effect, you may give this Character −2000 power during this turn instead.
[When Attacking] Give up to 1 of your opponent's Characters −2000 power during this turn.

---

## Lucky.Roux
**PRB02-003** · Character · Red

[Blocker] (After your opponent declares an attack, you may rest this card to make it the new target of the attack.)
[On Play] You may trash 1 Character card with 6000 power or more from your hand: Draw 2 cards.

---

## Jewelry Bonney
**PRB02-004** · Character · Green

[Blocker] (After your opponent declares an attack, you may rest this card to make it the new target of the attack.)
[On Your Opponent's Attack] [Once Per Turn] Set up to 1 of your DON!! cards as active.

---

## Monkey.D.Luffy
**PRB02-005** · Character · Green

[Your Turn] [On Play] If your Leader is multicolored and your opponent has 7 or less DON!! cards on their field, your opponent rests 1 of their active DON!! cards at the start of their next Main Phase.

---

## Roronoa Zoro
**PRB02-006** · Character · Green

[Opponent's Turn] If this Character would be rested by your opponent's Character's effect, you may rest 1 of your other Characters instead.
[Blocker]

---

## Jinbe
**PRB02-007** · Character · Blue

[On Play] Look at 5 cards from the top of your deck; reveal up to 1 {The Seven Warlords of the Sea} type card other than [Jinbe] and add it to your hand. Then, place the rest at the bottom of your deck in any order.
[When Attacking] Place up to 1 Character with a cost of 1 or less at the bottom of the owner's deck.

## Marco
**PRB02-008** · Character · Blue

[Blocker] (After your opponent declares an attack, you may rest this card to make it the new target of the attack.)
[On K.O.] Draw 2 cards.

## Mr.3(Galdino)

[Blocker]

## Charlotte Pudding
**PRB02-010** · Character · Purple

[On Play] DON!! −2: If your Leader has the {Big Mom Pirates} type and your opponent has 6 or more DON!! cards on their field, draw 2 cards. Then, play up to 1 {Big Mom Pirates} type Character card with 6000 to 8000 power from your hand.

## Donquixote Doflamingo
**PRB02-011** · Character · Purple

[Blocker]
[On Play] If your Leader is multicolored, add up to 1 DON!! card from your DON!! deck and rest it.

## Nami

**Trigger:** [Trigger] Play this card.

## Gecko Moria
**PRB02-013** · Character · Black

[On Play] If your Leader has the {Thriller Bark Pirates} type, play up to 1 Character card with a cost of 4 or less from your trash rested. Then, give up to 1 rested DON!! card to your Leader or 1 of your Characters.

## Sabo
**PRB02-014** · Character · Black

[Blocker] (After your opponent declares an attack, you may rest this card to make it the new target of the attack.)

## Shiryu
**PRB02-015** · Character · Black

If your Leader has the {Blackbeard Pirates} type, this Character gains [Blocker] and +4 cost.
[On K.O.] If your Leader has the {Blackbeard Pirates} type, K.O. up to 1 of your opponent's Characters with a base cost of 4 or less.

## Otama
**PRB02-016** · Character · Yellow

[Activate: Main] You may rest this Character and add 1 card from the top or bottom of your Life cards to your hand: Up to 1 of your Leader or Character cards gains +3000 power during this turn.

**Trigger:** [Trigger] Rest up to 1 of your opponent's Characters with a cost of 4 or less.

---

## Boa Hancock
**PRB02-017** · Character · Yellow

[On Play] You may trash 1 card with a [Trigger] from your hand: Your opponent's rested Leader or up to 1 of your opponent's Characters other than [Monkey.D.Luffy] cannot attack until the end of your opponent's next End Phase.

**Trigger:** [Trigger] K.O. up to 1 of your opponent's Characters with a cost of 4 or less.

---

## Portgas.D.Ace
**PRB02-018** · Character · Yellow

[On Play] If you have a face-up Life card, play up to 1 [Sabo], [Portgas.D.Ace], or [Monkey.D.Luffy] with a cost of 2 from your hand or trash.
