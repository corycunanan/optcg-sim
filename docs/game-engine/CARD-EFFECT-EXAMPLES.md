## Multi-Effect Cards
Vivi 
EB03-001 
[Once Per Turn] If your Character with a base cost of 4 or more would be K.O.'d, you may trash 1 card from your hand instead.
[Activate: Main] You may rest this Leader: Give up to 1 of your opponent's Characters −2000 power during this turn. Then, up to 1 of your Characters without a [When Attacking] effect gains [Rush] during this turn.

Lucy
OP15-002
[When Attacking]/[On Your Opponent's Attack] You may trash any number of Event or Stage cards from your hand. This Leader gains +1000 power during this battle for every card trashed.
[Activate: Main] [Once Per Turn] If you have activated an Event with a base cost of 3 or more during this turn, draw 1 card.
